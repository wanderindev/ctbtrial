Questions about controlling your CTB

How to control your CTB from Telegram

3 answers
How can I control my CTB?
10/19/2021

From Telegram you can control your CTB from anywhere at any time. For this purpose, you need to create a Telegram bot and provide us its access token and your user id. We will user this information to connect your CTB with your Telegram bot.

What can I do from Telegram on my CTB?
10/19/2021

You can query information from CTB such as your balance, what assets you currently hold, your profits, and so on. You can also stop your CTB at any time and restart it. Below is a list of commands you can send from Telegram.

Command Effect
/start Starts the trader.
/stop Stops the trader
/stopbuy Stops the trader from opening new trades. Gracefully closes open trades according to their rules.
/reload_config Reloads the configuration file.
/status Lists all open trades.
/status <trade_id> Lists one or more specific trade. Separate multiple with a blank space.
/status table List all open trades in a table format. Pending buy orders are marked with an asterisk (*). Pending sell orders are marked with a double asterisk (**).
/trades [limit] List all recently closed trades in a table format.
/count Displays number of trades used and available.
/profit [<n>] Display a summary of your profit/loss from close trades and some stats about your performance, over the last n days (all trades by default).
/forcesell <trade_id> Instantly sells the given trade (ignoring minimum_roi).
/forcesell all Instantly sells all open trades (ignoring minimum_roi).
/performance Show performance of each finished trade grouped by pair.
/balance Show account balance per currency.
/daily <n> Shows profit or loss per day, over the last n days (n defaults to 7).
/stats Shows wins/losses by sell reason as well as avg. holding durations for buys and sells.
/whitelist Show the current whitelist.

Questions about the strategy

A quick look at the main aspects of the strategy

7 answers
What assets can CTB buy?
10/19/2021

Currently, CTB can trade a specific list of 85 crypto assets. We picked those assets based on the trading volume in Binance and excluded all stable coins and BNB. Below is the current allowed pairs list.

Can the list of assets change?
10/19/2021

Yes. We review the performance and trade volume of assets in the exchange periodically. We remove assets from the allowed list that are underperforming and include those that are overperforming.

If I buy assets manually, will CTB try to sell them?
10/19/2021

No, CTB only sells the assets it bought.

What rules does CTB follow for buying/selling?
10/19/2021

CTB connects to Binance every 5 seconds and uses real-time market data for each the allowed assets to compute two technical analysis indicators: relative strength index (RSI) and Bollinger bands (BB). With the combined values for these indicators, CTB determines if an asset is undervalued or overvalued. If undervalued, it buys. If overvalued, it sells.

Can the rules change?
10/19/2021

For this trial, we will stick with the strategy using RSI and Bollinger bands. However, the exact value for these indicators CTB uses to determine if it should buy or sell will likely change every time we modify the allowed assets.

How do you determine the RSI and BB values the CTB will use?
10/19/2021

We fine-tune the strategy by finding optimal parameters through a process called hyperparameter optimization (hyperopt). The process starts with a random parameter combination. It then uses a Bayesian search with a machine learning regressor algorithm to find a parameter combination that produces maximum profits with minimum volatility.

We used special servers with 32 cores and 512 Gb of ram to run the optimizations. However, even with these powerful machines, the process takes hours because the search hyperspace is enormous, forcing us to run them for tens of thousands of epochs.

Every time an aspect of the strategy changes, for instance, modifying the allowed assets list, we rerun the hyperopt.

Can I get more details on the strategy and it backtests results?
10/19/2021

Sure, visit the strategy section to learn more.

Questions about funding your CTB

Running CTB with fake or real money

5 answers
Can I test CTB before committing any money?
10/19/2021

When we create your CTB, it will be running against real-time market data but using fake money. We call this a dry-run. You are encouraged to dry-run for as long as you want. If you ever decide to change to real money, just let us know, and we will make the switch for you. Note that after switching to real money, you will not be able to switch back to dry-run. You can, however, stop the bot at any time.

How much fake money is available for CTB?
10/19/2021

CTB starts with a dry-run wallet of $5,000 USDT.

What currency does CTB use to buy/sell crypto assets?
10/19/2021

In dry-run, CTB uses the USDT in your dry-run wallet. In production, it uses the USDT in your fiat/spot wallet.

How much USDT can I put into my Binance wallet?
10/19/2021

You can put as much as you want. We have no visibility into your account. However, we encourage you to start with a small amount and only increase it after you grasp how CTB is behaving against market conditions. Please, don't transfer money to Binance that you are afraid to lose.

How much of the USDT in my fiat/spot wallet does it use?
10/19/2021

It uses all of it. So, if the strategy generates a profit, CTB will also use those profits to buy crypto assets, producing a compounding effect. If you don't want CTB using your profits, you can convert them from USDT to USDC or any other stable coin since CTB doesn't use any stable coin other than USDT.
